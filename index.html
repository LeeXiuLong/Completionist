About the App

Wait for sale is an app designed to help you decide what games are the most worth it for the price. We have curated data from select popular games that we believe our users would be interested in buying. For the most optimally high value games please see the price filter labeled "$/Rating" and look towards the top right. The farther to the right a game is, the higher the rating. The farther up a game is, the cheaper the game. You can also select from a variety of filters including the genre of game, as well as the price point that you would like to select from if you're on a budget. Play around with the app and we hope that you save some money and bring more value to your time!
